Here you can find all the information you need to know about the villagers in my village. You can also find a gallery of my favorite villagers and my favorite items. Enjoy!

Admiral

Admiral is a cranky, blue octopus villager in the Animal Crossing series. He first appeared in Doubutsu no Mori e+ and has appeared in every game since. He is a jock villager, and his initial phrase is "aye aye." His English name is a reference to the rank of admiral in the military.

Alice

Alice is a peppy, pink rabbit villager in the Animal Crossing series. She first appeared in Doubutsu no Mori e+ and has appeared in every game since. She is a normal villager, and her initial phrase is "oh my."
